About

WELCOME TO QASWA HILLS RESTUARANT

Qaswa Hills Restaurant is a unique Arabic & Mughlai dining experience. Located in Vijayapura, by “Tahasildar’s”, the then revenue servants of Adil Shah Dynasty (few of the authentic title holders from badshaah). Mr Mehboob Tahasildar, have had a wealth of experience in restaurants. He is in hotel business since 1999. He is driven by a philosophy that incorporates the traditional values of exceptional service and the highest quality produce. They have consistently maintained Qaswa Hill’s commitment to quality.

HYGIENIC & EXCELLENT ATMOSPHERESS

We maintain hygiene as hygiene is most important thing. The venue has elegance inspired by the exquisite cuisine and the spectacular views. It has a huge dining hall, a very royal family dining hall section a separate juice & Ice cream parlor, besides a Air conditioned executive hall, the hotel really gives a fine dining royal experience.
